SMOOTHIES

PREMIUM AVAILABLE

Smoothies are a convenient and nutrient-dense way to support muscle building by providing a quick source of protein, healthy fats, and carbs for energy and recovery. They are easily customizable to include muscle-boosting ingredients like protein powder, fruits, nuts, and seeds.

Fruits:

Bananas (frozen for creaminess) • Mixed frozen berries (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries) • Apples (peeled and chopped)

Proteins:

Plant-based protein powder (vanilla or unflavored) • Chocolate plant-based protein powder • Vanilla protein powder • Protein powder (any type you prefer)

Dairy Alternatives:

Unsweetened almond milk • Unsweetened oat milk • Coconut yogurt (plain)

Seeds and Nuts:

Natural peanut butter or almond butter • Chia seeds • Flaxseeds • Ground flaxseeds • Almonds • Dates • Cacao nibs

Grains:

Rolled oats • Oatmeal (for smoothie use)

Other Items:

Cacao powder • Cinnamon • Maple syrup • Ice (optional) • Nutmeg

SUGGESTED RECIPES

Peanut Butter Banana Power Smoothie

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 large banana (frozen for creaminess)
    • 2 tablespoons natural peanut butter (or almond butter)
    • 1 scoop plant-based protein powder (vanilla or unflavored)
    • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
    • 1 teaspoon chia seeds
    • Optional: 1–2 dates for added sweetness
  • Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Add ice if you prefer a thicker texture.
  • Protein Boost: ~20–25g (depending on protein powder)

Chocolate Recovery Smoothie

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate plant-based protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon cacao powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 cup unsweetened oat milk
  • 1 teaspoon flaxseeds
  • Optional: A pinch of cinnamon for flavor
  • Instructions: Blend all ingredients. Top with cacao nibs or a sprinkle of cinnamon if desired.
  • Protein Boost: ~25g

      • Berry Blast Protein Smoothie

      • 1 cup mixed frozen berries (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries)
      • 1/2 cup plain coconut yogurt
      • 1 scoop vanilla plant-based protein powder
      • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
      • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
      • Optional: 1 teaspoon maple syrup for sweetness
    • Instructions: Blend until creamy and smooth. Garnish with a few fresh berries.
    • Protein Boost: ~20g

    Oatmeal Protein Smoothie

      • 1/2 cup rolled oats (soak in water or milk for 10 minutes)
      • 1 frozen banana, dates
      • 1 scoop protein powder
      • 1 cup almond milk
      • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
      • 1 cup milk (high protein content)
      • Optional: Dash of nutmeg or cinnamon
    • Instructions: Blend all ingredients into a creamy smoothie. Adjust milk for consistency.
    • Protein Boost: ~25g

      Apple Cinnamon Protein Smoothie

      • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
      • 1 apple (peeled and chopped)
      • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
      • 1 tbsp ground flaxseeds
      • 1 cup almond or normal milk
      • Ice (optional)
    • Directions: Blend all ingredients together until smooth. Apples provide fiber and natural sweetness, while cinnamon helps regulate blood sugar levels.

    ADDITIONAL TIPS FOR SMOOTHIES

    • Protein-Packed Smoothies: Use whey or plant-based protein powder, Greek yogurt, or nut butters to support muscle growth.
    • Perfect Timing: Drink a protein smoothie during your eating window or post-workout for optimal recovery.
    • Boost Nutrients: Add chia seeds, flaxseeds, leafy greens, and frozen fruits for extra vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants.
    • Healthy Fats: Include avocado, coconut oil, or nut butters for energy and healthy fats.
    • Hydration: Use water or unsweetened almond milk as your base liquid for hydration without excess calories.
    • Enhance with Supplements: Add creatine, BCAAs, or collagen for muscle recovery and joint health.
    • Sweetness: Use natural sweeteners like dates or stevia to add flavor without the sugar spike.
