Mid -snacks

PREMIUM AVAILABLE

Healthy snacks are perfect for fueling your fitness journey, providing sustained energy and essential nutrients. Choose options like nuts, Greek yogurt, boiled eggs, or whole-grain crackers with hummus. They’re easy to prepare, portable, and help curb hunger between meals!

Proteins:

Hard-boiled eggs • Greek yogurt (plain or flavored) • Cottage cheese (low-fat) • Turkey or chicken slices

Seeds and Nuts:

Raw almonds • Walnuts • Cashews • Sunflower seeds • Trail mix (unsweetened) • Nut butter (peanut, almond, or cashew)

Fruits:

Apples • Bananas • Oranges • Grapes • Dried fruits (unsweetened, like raisins or apricots) • Mixed berries

Grains:

Whole-grain crackers • Brown rice cakes • Popcorn (air-popped) • Whole-grain bread

Vegetables:

Carrot sticks • Celery sticks • Cucumber slices • Cherry tomatoes • Bell pepper strips

Other Items:

Hummus • Guacamole • Dark chocolate (70% cacao or higher) • Cheese sticks • Yogurt-covered nuts (unsweetened)

SUGGESTED RECIPES

Option 1:

    • Greek yogurt (200g) with mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts) and a handful of raisins or dates.

Option 2:

  • Chana chaat (boiled chickpeas, tomatoes, onions, cucumber, lemon, and spices)
  • A small piece of fruit (mango, papaya, or apple).

    Option 3:

    • Protein smoothie with whey protein (1 scoop), banana, oats (30g), and almond milk or regular milk.

Option 4:

    • Boiled eggs (2-3 egg whites) with a sprinkle of salt and pepper.
    • Paneer tikka (grilled, without oil) with a cucumber salad.

    • • Sweet potato (100 g)
    • 2 bread with peanut butter and 1 banana
